# Elegance Salon Website

A professional, modern website for Elegance Salon - a premier hair care and styling establishment.

## Project Overview

Elegance Salon Website is a fully responsive, multi-page web application showcasing salon services, gallery, and contact information. The website is built with clean HTML, CSS, and organized folder structure for easy maintenance and scalability.

## Pages Description

### Home (index.html)
- Welcome hero section with call-to-action button
- Quick links to all main sections
- Navigation to services, gallery, about, and contact pages

### Services (pages/services.html)
- Complete list of salon services:
  - Hair Cutting
  - Hair Coloring
  - Hair Styling
  - Treatments
  - Hair Washing
  - Blow Dry

### Gallery (pages/gallery.html)
- Professional photo gallery
- Showcase of salon work and facilities
- 6 high-quality images with descriptions

### About Us (pages/about.html)
- Salon history and mission
- Team information
- Why Choose Us section with key benefits
- Certified stylists information

### Contact (pages/contact.html)
- Phone and email contact information
- Physical location and address
- Business hours:
  - Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM
  - Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  - Sunday: Closed
- Appointment booking information

## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Desktop**: Full layout (1200px+)
- **Tablet**: Grid adjustments (768px - 1199px)
- **Mobile**: Single column layout (<768px)
- **Small Mobile**: Further optimized fonts and spacing (<480px)

## How to Use

1. **Open the website**: Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Navigate**: Use the menu at the top to navigate between pages
3. **Book an Appointment**: Click "Book an Appointment" button to go to contact page
4. **View Services**: Click on service cards to learn more or navigate via menu
5. **Explore Gallery**: View salon photos in the Gallery section
6. **Contact**: Find phone, email, and location information on Contact page

- Keep the same filenames or update paths in `pages/gallery.html`

All styles are in `css/styles.css`:
- Change colors in color variables
- Adjust spacing and sizing in respective sections
- Modify responsive breakpoints at the bottom
